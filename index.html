Crafting Confident Voices — ECE B Toastmasters Club

A sanctuary for the bold, a stage for the aspiring. ECE B Toastmasters is a student-run public speaking and leadership club established in 2026, part of the official Toastmasters International network.

We meet regularly to practice communication skills, take on structured speaking roles, and grow as confident leaders. Our platform helps members view and manage their meeting schedule, track attendance, and stay connected.

Our Mission

To provide a mutually supportive and positive learning environment in which every individual member has the opportunity to develop oral communication and leadership skills, which foster self-confidence and personal growth.

Our Vision

To be the first-choice provider of dynamic, high-value, experiential communication and leadership skills development.

Club at a Glance

  • 60 active member voices
  • 15 structured speaking roles per meeting
  • Weekly meetings with role rotation and attendance tracking
  • Icebreaker games and themed sessions
